Albert Einstein

He is best known for developing the theory of relativity

Here's a time line of Einstein's life:

  • 1879 - he was born at Ulm, in Württemberg, Germany
  • 1896 - he entered the Swiss Federal Polytechnic School in Zurich to be trained as a teacher in physics and mathematics
  • 1901 - he gained his diploma, he acquired Swiss citizenship and, as he was unable to find a teaching post, he accepted a position as technical assistant in the Swiss Patent Office
  • 1905 - he obtained his doctor’s degree
  • 1908 - he was appointed Privatdozent in Berne
  • 1909 - he became Professor Extraordinary at Zurich
  • 1911 - he became Professor of Theoretical Physics at Prague, returning to Zurich in the following year to fill a similar post
  • 1914 - he was appointed Director of the Kaiser Wilhelm Physical Institute and Professor at the University of Berlin. Also, he became a german citizen
  • 1933 - he renounced his citizenship for political reasons and immigrated to America to take the position of Professor of Theoretical Physics at Princeton
  • 1940 - he became a United States citizen
  • 1945 - hr retired from his post
  • 1955 - he died at Princeton, New Jersey
